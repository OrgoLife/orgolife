Coratina Olive Oil

The olive tree is a fruit tree, just like the mango, and there are many different varieties of olives, each variety has its own advantages and different characteristics, and this results in a difference in the taste, color, and odor of olive oil.

Quick Information: Coratina

  • The tree is of Italian origin
  • It has a robust flavor and a peppery after taste
  • It has a pleasant aroma

Suitable For people who are:

  • Love olive oil
  • Enjoy the powerful, pungent and peppery taste of olive oil
  • Olive oil enthusiasts who enjoy a rich Flavor profile

Applications:

  • Drizzle over salads, pasta and vegetables.
  • Marinades or sauces for meat, fish or poultry.
  • Replace Butter or Ghee in your recipes 

About Us

  • We are a small farm located in Sinai.
  • Out of all the olive varieties in our farm we picked 3 to press and extract olive oil from, and you happen to hold a Coratina olive oil bottle in your hands.

About our Olive Oil

  • Extra virgin olive oil Single Variety:  Coratina

  • Acidity less than 0.8% and can be as low as 0.5%

  • Olive oil pressed from very carefully selected olives in order to produce the best olive oil in terms of taste and nutritional value
  • Cold pressed in the right way: without turning on the heaters at all or adding water while slowing the kneading process so as not to raise the temperature of the olives due to friction
  • High concentration of phenolic compounds giving it a pungent and peppery aftertaste.
