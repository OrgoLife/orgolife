About our Olive Oil

  • Extra virgin olive oil Single Variety: Coratina, Picual, Frontoio
  • Acidity less than 0.5%
  • Olive oil pressed from very carefully selected olives in order to produce the best olive oil in terms of taste and nutritional value
  • Cold pressed in the right way: without turning on the heaters at all or adding water while slowing the kneading process so as not to raise the temperature of the olives due to friction
  • High concentration of phenolic compounds giving it a pungent and peppery aftertaste.
